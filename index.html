    Steven Kong

    Hey! My name is Steven Kong, a software engineering student currently studying at the University of Waterloo. I was born and raised in Montreal, Quebec. When I was a kid, playing with my LEGO© sets as I was deathly afraid of sports, I just wanted to build cool things and, although it may sound simplistic, I've made that my dream as an aspiring programmer. Among the things I've tried so far, a few things have stuck with me. I enjoy cooking, but whether what I make is edible is another thing. I attempt to take nice pictures. I desperately struggle to stay fit even though I go back to square zero everytime I pass a Chipotle. I find entertainment in hitting a ball back and forth with a racquet. I can play the piano. And, of course, I waste time on video games (perhaps a bit too much). That sums me up today but hopefully I'll wake up just a little bit better tomorrow!
